Why does it cost so much to learn math?
We think parents, students, schools and teachers pay way too much money for what's out there.
  • Gaphing calculators currently on the market have seen little technological innovation since their debut in 1990.
  • Current standardized tests only allow use of graphing calculators that are too expensive for students for low-income backgrounds to access. We want to change this.
